About Drawing

Drawing is a form of visual art where an artist creates images using lines, shapes, and textures. It can be done with various tools like pencils, pens, charcoal, ink, pastels, or digital devices.

Tips for Learning Drawing

  • Practice basic lines and shapes.
  • understand light and shadow.
  • Observe objects closely.
  • Experiment with different mediums.
  • Draw regularly to improve skills.

The reason I like to draw is:

1. Self-Expression – Drawing allows you to express emotions, thoughts, and ideas visually.

2. Creativity – It helps bring imagination to life, creating unique and original artworks.

3. Relaxation & Stress Relief – Many find drawing calming and meditative, helping to reduce stress.

4. Skill Development – Improving artistic abilities and mastering new techniques can be satisfying.

5. Storytelling – Art can convey messages, emotions, and narratives without words.

6. Escape from Reality – Drawing can transport you to a different world, offering a break from daily life.
